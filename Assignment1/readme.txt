Steps to run the code

1. Open the terminal and point to the current directory
2. type "python assignment1.py"
3. You will on the terminal the 3 probabilites of the sentences
4. In the same directory you will see 8 new txt files:
    a) addOne1.txt and addOne2.txt give you the addone word probability of the two sentences respectively
    b) countTable1.txt and countTable2.txt give you the counts of the bigrams of the two sentences respectively
    c) unsmooth1.txt and unsmooth2.txt give you the unsmooth word probability of the two sentences respectively
    d) goodTuring1.txt and goodTuring2.txt give you the goodTuring word probability of the two sentences respectively
